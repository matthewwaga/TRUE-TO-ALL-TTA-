Our Story

Named after the one thing a hang-tag can't fake.

True To All started in Nairobi with a simple complaint: too many labels sell a story and quietly cut the fabric weight to match the discount. We wanted the opposite — clothes where the tag tells you exactly what you're getting, and the garment backs it up.

Every TTA piece is designed, priced, and printed with that in mind. GSM, fiber content, and stitch details go on the listing, not just the marketing copy. If a piece changes between seasons, the tag changes with it.

We build for the city first — Kilimani, Ngong Road, Karura, the matatu routes that get you there — and price in KES because that's who's actually buying.
